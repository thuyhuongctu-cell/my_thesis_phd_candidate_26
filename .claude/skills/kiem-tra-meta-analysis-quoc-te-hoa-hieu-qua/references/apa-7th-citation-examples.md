(2018) conducted a meta-analysis of 43 independent samples.\n```\n\n**With page number:**\n```\nAs Contractor et al. (2003) noted, \"the costs of coordination increase exponentially\" (p. 15).\n```\n\n---\n\n## Common Errors and Corrections\n\n### Error 1: Incorrect DOI Format\n❌ `doi:10.1177/1042258718795346`\n❌ `http://dx.doi.org/10.1177/1042258718795346`\n✅ `https://doi.org/10.1177/1042258718795346`\n\n### Error 2: Capitalization in Titles\n❌ `The Effect Of Context-Related Moderators On The Internationalization-Performance Relationship`\n✅ `The effect of context-related moderators on the internationalization-performance relationship`\n\nNote: Capitalize after colon:\n✅ `Meta-analysis: A comprehensive review`\n\n### Error 3: Ampersand vs. \"And\"\n❌ Parenthetical: `(Smith and Jones, 2020)`\n✅ Parenthetical: `(Smith & Jones, 2020)`\n❌ Narrative: `Smith & Jones (2020) found...`\n✅ Narrative: `Smith and Jones (2020) found...`\n\n### Error 4: Et al. Usage\n❌ First citation: `(Smith, Jones, Brown, & Lee, 2020)`\n✅ First citation: `(Smith et al., 2020)` [for 3+ authors in 7th ed.]\n\n### Error 5: Issue Number Italics\n❌ `Journal Name, 47(3), 319-347.` [issue in italics]\n✅ `Journal Name, 47(3), 319-347.` [only volume in italics]\n\n### Error 6: Retrieval Dates\n❌ `Retrieved January 15, 2024, from https://...` [for stable sources]\n✅ No retrieval date for journal articles, books with DOI\n✅ Use retrieval date only for content that may change (wikis, unarchived webpages)\n\n### Error 7: Database Names in References\n❌ Include database name for published journal articles\n✅ Only include database for dissertations/theses\n\n---\n\n## Quick Reference Table\n\n| Element | Format | Example |\n|---------|--------|---------|\n| Journal volume | Italics | *47*(3) |\n| Journal issue | No italics | 47*(3)* → 47(3) |\n| DOI | https://doi.org/... | https://doi.org/10.1007/s11575-007-0019-z |\n| Et al. | From first citation | (Smith et al., 2020) |\n| Ampersand | In parentheses & references | (Smith & Jones, 2020) |\n| \"And\" | In narrative citations | Smith and Jones (2020) |\n| Page numbers | p. or pp. | (Smith, 2020, p. 15) |\n| Multiple pages | pp. with en dash | (Smith, 2020, pp. 15-17) |\n| Issue number | Only if paginated by issue | 47(3) [omit if continuous pagination] |\n| Book edition | In parentheses | (2nd ed.) |\n| Book location | Omit in 7th ed. | Publisher only, no city |
