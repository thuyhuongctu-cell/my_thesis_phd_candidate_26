# Journal-Specific Guidelines for Top IB Journals

Các tạp chí hàng đầu trong International Business có yêu cầu và phong cách riêng. Tài liệu này tóm tắt đặc điểm của các tạp chí chính.

## Journal of International Business Studies (JIBS)

**Ranking:** #1 trong IB (ABDC A*, ABS 4*, FT50)

**Scope:**
- Broad IB topics: internationalization, MNCs, cross-border activities, global strategy
- Paradigmatically và methodologically pluralistic
- Yêu cầu strong theoretical contribution

**Submission Requirements:**
- **Length:** 10,000 words cho major contributions; 7,000 words cho narrower contributions
- **Word count includes:** Abstract, text, endnotes, references (tables và figures không tính)
- **Abstract:** Không quy định cụ thể, nhưng thường 150-200 words
- **Citation style:** APA-based, nhưng có JIBS Style Guide riêng
- **Format:** Double-spaced, 12pt Times New Roman

**Special Features:**
- **Data Access and Research Transparency (DART) Policy:** Khuyến khích data sharing
- **Research Notes:** 4,000-6,000 words cho narrower contributions
- **Perspectives:** Conceptual/theoretical papers không có empirical data

**What JIBS Values:**
- Theoretical rigor và novelty
- Methodological sophistication
- Clear contribution to IB literature
- International/cross-border focus (không phải chỉ single-country studies)

**Common Rejection Reasons:**
- Insufficient theoretical contribution
- Limited international dimension
- Spurious citations to JIBS articles (cite vì cite, không vì relevant)
- Manuscript quá dài mà contribution hẹp

**Typical Review Time:** 3-6 months

**Acceptance Rate:** ~8-10%

**Recent Trends (2024-2026):**
- Tăng focus vào sustainability và digitalization trong IB
- Quan tâm đến EMNEs và South-South FDI
- Methodological pluralism: qualitative, mixed methods được chào đón

## International Business Review (IBR)

**Ranking:** ABDC A, ABS 3

**Scope:**
- Broad IB topics với focus vào empirical studies
- Marketing và management issues trong international context
- Theoretical và methodological developments

**Submission Requirements:**
- **Length:** 8,000 words maximum (excluding references và tables)
- **Abstract:** 150 words maximum
- **Citation style:** Harvard/APA hybrid (author-year in-text)
- **Format:** Double-spaced

**What IBR Values:**
- Empirical rigor
- Practical relevance
- Clear research questions
- Both quantitative và qualitative methods

**Special Notes:**
- Không yêu cầu "correct format" cho initial submission (có thể submit single PDF)
- Chỉ format properly khi revise & resubmit
- Khuyến khích graphical abstracts

**Typical Review Time:** 2-4 months

**Acceptance Rate:** ~15-20%

## Journal of World Business (JWB)

**Ranking:** ABDC A, ABS 3, FT50

**Scope:**
- Strategy và management của global firms
- Emerging markets
- Global value chains
- International entrepreneurship

**Submission Requirements:**
- **Length:** 10,000 words (including everything)
- **Abstract:** 150 words
- **Citation style:** APA
- **Format:** Double-spaced, 12pt

**What JWB Values:**
- Managerial relevance
- Emerging market contexts
- Phenomenon-driven research
- Clear implications for practice

**Special Features:**
- **Special Issues:** Thường có themed special issues
- **Invited Editorials:** Thought leadership pieces

**Typical Review Time:** 3-5 months

**Acceptance Rate:** ~12-15%

---

## Management International Review (MIR)

**Ranking:** ABDC A, ABS 3

**Scope:**
- International management
- Cross-cultural management
- Comparative management
- European perspective on IB

**Submission Requirements:**
- **Length:** 10,000-12,000 words
- **Abstract:** 150-200 words
- **Citation style:** APA
- **Format:** Double-spaced

**What MIR Values:**
- European và non-US contexts
- Cross-cultural research
- Institutional perspectives
- Comparative studies

**Typical Review Time:** 3-6 months

**Acceptance Rate:** ~10-12%

## Global Strategy Journal (GSJ)

**Ranking:** ABDC A, ABS 3

**Scope:**
- Global strategy

**Submission Requirements:**
- **Length:** 12,000 words (including everything)
- **Abstract:** 150 words
- **Citation style:** APA

**What GSJ Values:**
- Strategy focus (không phải pure IB)

## Chiến Lược Chọn Journal

### 1. Đánh giá contribution của bạn

**JIBS nếu:**
- Strong theoretical contribution
- Novel theoretical framework hoặc significant extension
- Multi-country, cross-border focus
- Sophisticated methodology

**IBR nếu:**
- Solid empirical study
- Clear practical implications
- Interesting context (emerging markets, specific industries)
- Good fit với recent IBR publications

**JWB nếu:**
- Managerial relevance cao
- Emerging market context
- Phenomenon-driven (interesting business phenomenon)
- Strategic management angle

**MIR nếu:**
- Cross-cultural hoặc comparative focus
- European context
- Institutional analysis
- Cultural dimensions

**GSJ nếu:**
- Strategy focus mạnh
- Global competitive advantage
- Firm-level strategy trong international context

### 2. Đọc recent issues

Trước khi submit, đọc 10-15 bài gần nhất của tạp chí để hiểu:
- Typical paper structure
- Common methodologies
- Tone và style
- Types of contributions được publish

### 3. Check Aims & Scope carefully

Mỗi tạp chí có statement về loại bài họ muốn. Đảm bảo bài của bạn fit.

### 4. Consider desk rejection risk

Desk rejection (reject không qua review) xảy ra khi:
- Bài không fit scope
- Contribution quá yếu
- Methodology có vấn đề nghiêm trọng
- Writing quality quá kém
- Bài quá dài/ngắn

**Để tránh desk rejection:**
- Follow formatting guidelines chặt chẽ
- Write clear và compelling introduction
- Articulate contribution rõ ràng ngay từ đầu
- Ensure bài đủ "international" cho IB journals

## Citation Styles: APA vs. Harvard trong IB

### APA Style (American Psychological Association)

**In-text citation:**
```
Single author: (Dunning, 1988)
Two authors: (Kogut & Singh, 1988)
Three or more: (Meyer et al., 2020)
```

**Reference list:**
```
Journal article:
Johanson, J., & Vahlne, J.-E. (1977). The internationalization process of the firm: A model of knowledge development and increasing foreign market commitments. Journal of International Business Studies, 8(1), 23-32.

Book:
Dunning, J. H. (1993). Multinational enterprises and the global economy. Addison-Wesley.

Edited book chapter:
Kostova, T., & Roth, K. (2002). Adoption of an organizational practice by subsidiaries of multinational corporations. In C. Cooper & I. Robertson (Eds.), International review of industrial and organizational psychology (pp. 215-249). Wiley.
```

### Harvard Style

**In-text citation:**
```
Single author: (Dunning 1988)
Two authors: (Kogut and Singh 1988)
Three or more: (Meyer et al. 2020)
```

**Reference list:**
```
Journal article:
Johanson, J and Vahlne, J-E (1977) 'The internationalization process of the firm', Journal of International Business Studies, 8(1), pp. 23-32.

Book:
Dunning, JH (1993) Multinational enterprises and the global economy, Addison-Wesley, Reading, MA.
```

**Key differences:**
- APA dùng "&", Harvard dùng "and"
- APA có comma trước year, Harvard không
- APA dùng sentence case cho titles, Harvard có thể dùng title case
- Harvard thường thêm location cho books

**Lưu ý:** JIBS có slight variations từ standard APA. Luôn check journal's specific style guide.

## Tips for Responding to Reviewers

Khi bạn nhận được Revise & Resubmit (R&R):

### 1. Đọc kỹ tất cả comments
- Đọc editor's letter trước
- Đọc từng reviewer comment
- Identify major vs. minor concerns

### 2. Tạo response letter có cấu trúc

**Template:**
```
Dear Editor [Name],

Thank you for the opportunity to revise our manuscript. We appreciate the constructive feedback from the reviewers. Below, we address each comment in detail.

[Summary of major changes]

REVIEWER 1

Comment 1.1: [Quote exact comment]

Response: [Your response]

Changes made: [Specific changes in manuscript, with page/line numbers]

Comment 1.2: ...

[Continue for all comments]

REVIEWER 2
...

We believe these revisions have significantly strengthened the manuscript. Thank you again for the opportunity to revise.

Sincerely,
[Authors]
```

### 3. Address EVERY comment

Kể cả khi bạn disagree:
- Acknowledge the concern
- Explain your reasoning politely
- Offer compromise nếu possible

**Ví dụ:**
```
Comment: "The sample size is too small."

Response: We appreciate the reviewer's concern about sample size. While our sample of 150 firms is smaller than some large-scale studies, it is comparable to other qualitative-quantitative mixed methods research in this domain (cite examples). Moreover, our focus on in-depth case analysis requires this more manageable sample size to ensure data quality. We have added a discussion of this limitation in the revised manuscript (p. 28, lines 456-462).
```

### 4. Highlight changes in manuscript

- Use track changes hoặc highlight
- Provide line numbers trong response letter
- Make it easy cho reviewers to see changes

### 5. Tone professional và respectful

- Tránh defensive tone
- Thank reviewers cho insights
- Acknowledge khi they caught genuine issues

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Submitting to wrong journal:** Bài về domestic business submitted to IB journal

2. **Ignoring word limits:** JIBS sẽ desk reject nếu vượt quá 10,000 words đáng kể

3. **Poor abstract:** Abstract là first impression, phải compelling

4. **Weak contribution statement:** Không rõ ràng về contribution

5. **Insufficient literature review:** Không engage với IB literature đầy đủ

6. **Methodology issues:** Thiếu chi tiết, validity/reliability không rõ

7. **Over-claiming:** Claiming contributions quá lớn so với actual findings

8. **Ignoring limitations:** Không thừa nhận limitations hoặc dismiss chúng

9. **Poor English:** Grammar và spelling errors nhiều

10. **Formatting errors:** Không follow journal guidelines
