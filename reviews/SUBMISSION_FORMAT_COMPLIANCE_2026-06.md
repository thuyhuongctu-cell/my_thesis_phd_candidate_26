## Notes
- **Reference style:** Emerald packages use Emerald-Harvard (converted). Springer/Elsevier/Wiley/T&F packages use APA author–date, accepted at initial submission under each publisher's *Your Paper Your Way* / free-format-submission policy.
- **Highlights:** required by Elsevier only (3–5 points, ≤85 characters each); present on all five Elsevier packages.
- **Ethics:** secondary, de-identified public WBES data; statement included where the publisher requires it (Springer, Wiley). Not applicable for Emerald/Elsevier/T&F.
- **CRediT / ORCID:** on the non-blinded title page (`02_title_page.md`) to preserve manuscript anonymity.
- **Abstract:** structured (Purpose/Design/Findings/Originality) for Emerald; unstructured for all other publishers.
