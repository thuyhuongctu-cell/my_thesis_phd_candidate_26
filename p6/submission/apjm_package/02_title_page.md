## Sample and Method

- Three-level meta-analytic regression analysis (MARA) of the internationalization–performance (I–P) relationship
- *k* = 238 coded studies; *K* = 288 effect sizes; 45 economies; four decades (1977–2025)
- Pre-registered on OSF (https://osf.io/z37kn; DOI: 10.17605/OSF.IO/Z37KN; registered May 18, 2026)
- Single-coder extraction with double-entry verification (transparently disclosed in §3.3.2 and §5 Limitations)
