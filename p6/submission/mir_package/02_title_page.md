## Data Availability Statement

The coded study database, R replication scripts (*metafor*) with an independent Python cross-check, the PRISMA flow diagram, and the extraction codebook are openly available via the project's Open Science Framework (OSF) record: https://osf.io/z37kn (DOI: 10.17605/OSF.IO/Z37KN). The OSF pre-registration was lodged prior to effect-size extraction. Per MIR's open-science encouragement, the extended methodological appendices (PRISMA 2020 trace; full 7-moderator coding protocol; MetaEssentials-versus-*metafor* consistency check) are deposited on OSF as supplementary materials accompanying this submission.

## Ethics Statement

This research is a meta-analysis of previously published, anonymized aggregate effect sizes from secondary sources. No primary data collection from human or animal subjects was conducted; institutional review board approval was therefore not required.
