## Why JABS (Asia / South-Asia fit)

JABS focuses on management and business scholarship in Asian contexts. India provides a South Asian, democratic, federal, common-law institutional environment under active developmental-state policy (Atmanirbhar Bharat, PLI), theoretically distinct from the East Asian transition-economy evidence that dominates the emerging-market I-P literature. The UPI quasi-experiment (Asia's largest digital public infrastructure) and the South Asian SME global-value-chain inclusion analysis are of direct regional relevance.
