## 3. Pooled level effects (DAI vs TCI, both controlled)

- DAI (website): +0.241 (p=0.0000)
- TCI (R&D+ISO): +0.124 (p=0.0000)
- N = 80900

## Interpretation

Digital adoption pays in EVERY regime (all p<.05) but the premium is largest in catching-up regimes (transition, resource) and compressed in the highest-adoption advanced regime — the digital-saturation pattern. The lowest-adoption regimes (Emerging, SIDS) still earn a significant premium, so closing the pre-AI digital divide there would yield real productivity gains.
